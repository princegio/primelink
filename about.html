About Us

Primelink Global Industries Inc. is a trading company established in 2019 and a subsidiary of MD Tambungui Specialists (MDTS) Inc. MDTS Inc. is a general contractor with ongoing outside plant projects and maintenance works for various telecommunication companies. With the rising need to further innovate and keep up with the trend in technology, the company has decided to put up Primelink Global Industries, a separate entity that would cater a contractor’s need in tools, equipment and hardware supplies. Primelink Global Industries aims to bring a quality tools and equipment that aid contractors in their respective field.

OBJECTIVE
Our company’s objective is to promote a profitable and sustainable business activity without compromising our customer’s satisfaction and needs.

MISSION
We are committed to provide our clients competitive and premium quality equipment and services.

VISION
We aim to be the leading supplier of various telecommunication and other utility tools, equipment and hardware supplies in the Philippines.
